About me


Hi, I'm Hannah! I'm a third year undergraduate student at the University of Pittsburgh studying physics and astronomy and minoring in computer science. I have the privilege of working on astronomy research with Dr. Evan Schneider. My research focuses on the significance of resolution in cloud-wind interaction simulations in order to better understand the multiphase nature of galactic winds. I hope to continue doing astronomy research by pursuing a PhD after graduation.

Outside of academics, I like to run, rock climb, and crochet. My favorite thing to do is draw and paint, and you can view some of my artwork here!
